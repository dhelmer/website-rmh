I produced a product called ‘Dry Magic’ which drew questions from countries from around the world. In the spring of 1964 I decided to go around the world to talk to the interested organizations in person. I met my friend John in Holland and he talked me into going to Hamburg to initiate my travels. My demonstration of German is a failure. My visit to an unusual club resuts in disappointment and a hangover of major proportions.
